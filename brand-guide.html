MEGAPARTNERS BRAND GUIDE

The MEGAPARTNERS brand exists for the continuous development and promotion of its own products in the field of iGaming. Our players and our partners, whose satisfaction has become our compass, are our main support in this matter.

    There should no distortion of color in the sign for keeping recognizability. Any modification negatively influences the brand identity. You cannot change the color of individual elements of the sign, use gradients, strokes and patterns.
